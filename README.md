# hong-kong-jockey-club-horse-racing-data-eda

## Introduction
My EDA project analyzes the horseracing results in Hong Kong. It is a biweekly event held every Wednesday and Saturday and serves as a big significant source and pastime for the local community, particularly among retired folks. I chose this topic because I realized betting on these horses involves more than mere luck after chatting with my sister's Father-In-Law after my recent trip back home.

I wanted to draw relationships between the pairing of trainers/jockeys and their horses and identify a few factors like lane position or horse weight that can predict results of the race. I had a hunch that lane position and the pairing of trainers and jockeys have a positive correlation on the placement (results).

## Data
I have gathered data on horse racing in Hong Kong from the official Hong Kong Jockey Club website. This is the racing results of all races in 2024 up until the start of my EDA (1/1/24 - 2/4/24).
